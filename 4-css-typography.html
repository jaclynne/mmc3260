Oh hey there!

My name is Jo-Anne and I'm a student at the University of Florida. I'm majoring in journalism and with a focus in online media. I love writing and I love the internet so I figured, why not put the two together?

Ever since I could remember, I’ve always loved reading and writing. I would make up stories about animals or people who got sucked into distant lands. As I grew, so did my stories. Now instead of one or two page stories, I’m currently in the middle of writing a book.

I enjoy journalistic writing as well. It’s always interesting to see what happened to who and when. What people think or have to say about a certain topic or idea has always interested me. Like I said before, I really love the internet. There are just so many things that people can connect with one another because of the internet.

If I could combine writing stories for readers and working on the internet, that would make me happy.
